Sustainable landscaping is practical in Colorado. It can reduce water demand, lower maintenance needs, and create outdoor spaces that hold up through changing weather.

## Smarter Materials

Native and adapted plants, stone mulch, efficient irrigation, and properly built hardscapes all support long-term performance.

## Everyday Benefits

A resilient landscape can look clean and intentional while using fewer resources and requiring less reactive upkeep.
