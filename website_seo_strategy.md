# Hawthorn Scapes Web & SEO Strategy

This strategy is tailored for Hawthorn Scapes, LLC to deliver a fast, professional website with minimal maintenance and strong local SEO coverage from the Boulder Area to Colorado Springs.

## Tech Stack

- Framework: Astro (static-first, fast by default)
- Deployment: Netlify or Cloudflare Pages
- Forms: Netlify Forms (`data-netlify="true"`)

## Service Grouping

Use a three-group layout to keep the homepage clean and skimmable.

### Lawn & Maintenance

- Professional mowing with edging and trimming
- Seasonal spring and fall clean-ups
- Stump removal and precision shrub trimming

### Installation & Design

- Tree and shrub installation using Colorado-hardy species
- Water-efficient xeriscaping for low-maintenance curb appeal
- Custom landscape design and full installation

### Hardscape & Structures

- Retaining walls and stone features for slope management
- Durable, residential fencing for privacy and security

## Local SEO Notes

- Include `LandscapingService` JSON-LD on the homepage
- List service cities across the I-25 corridor
- Keep NAP (name, address, phone) consistent across site and profiles
- Add project imagery over time, including macro texture shots for premium look

## Content Production Tip

When full-property photos are limited, use close-up shots of materials, plants, and detailing. This keeps visuals premium while your portfolio grows.
